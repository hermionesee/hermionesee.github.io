One Million Campaign

Introduction

  • Overview

    I had to design a 150x150m map in RPG Map Editor 2, featuring a chosen setting, background, and story or era. The project required a detailed Experience Draft that outlined the quest structure and resource distribution, ensuring that players could accumulate a total of 1,000,000 units of a specific resource through key interaction events within the level.

  • Story Context

    You awaken in a dense, mist-laced forest with no memory of where you are or why you are here, but you know one thing: Vengeance.
    With only your name, your broken memories and the consistent whisper of what seems to be your only purpose, you set forth towards the tower the voices are leading you towards

Map Overview

The map features seven main areas where players encounter key story events, each contributing to the overall progression of the game. The journey begins in the Edge Forest, serving as the player’s starting point and setting the premise for the unfolding narrative.

Map Flow

From the starting point at Edge Forest, the player will traverse through the different points on the map.

Edge Forest ➜ Memory Garden Loop ➜ Forgotten Residence ➜ Spirit Hollows ➜ Echoing Fields ➜ Tower

Storytelling

Drawing inspiration from the game "Ten Trials of Babel: Doppleganger Maze", I developed my storyline around themes of amnesia and lost identity, recurring memories, and an obsession with a singular purpose or emotion. The narrative is further shaped by the idea of being guided by fragmented memories of the past, creating a mysterious and emotionally driven experience for the player.
