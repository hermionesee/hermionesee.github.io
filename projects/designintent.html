My Design Intent

Introduction

  • Overview

    My Design Intent is an overview of a few projects that I have done during my Level Design module in DigiPen Institute of Technology Europe Bilbao. It serves as a form of portfolio showcasing a rough design process of my level designs.

  • Design Scheme

    My favourite colours are mostly blues and shades of pastel, thus the colour scheme of the document itself. I am a lover of doodle-kind of aesthetic and added elements that look like hand drawn as "stickers" pasted onto my own person portfolio, along with the font choice as well.

Cover Page

With the cover page being the first page the reader will see, I wanted to incorporate the overall theme that I was going to use within the portfolio. I aimed to show a playful and cheerful kind of energy when first viewing the portfolio.

Chosen Projects

With the page limit to 5 pages, I placed 3 of my chosen projects within the portfolio, along with a cover page and a page for a short introduction summary about myself.
